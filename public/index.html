KLECET Chikodi - Campus & Club

Official leadership profiles and placement wing overview.

KLE College of Engineering & Technology

KLE College of Engineering and Technology (KLECET), Chikodi was established in the year 2008 under the umbrella of the prestigious KLE Society, Belagavi. Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by AICTE, KLECET Chikodi offers state-of-the-art undergraduate programs in Computer Science, Electronics, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering.

The Training & Placement Cell

The Placement wing at KLECET acts as a robust bridge connecting engineering students with global IT recruiters. Through regular industry interactions, coding camps, and soft skill workshops organized in collaboration with the club, KLECET places students in top MNCs including TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Capgemini, Cognizant, and IBM.

The Literary & Coding Club Wing

Our Literary Club (Tech Wing) is dedicated to building the "Complete Engineer". We believe that strong technical coding skills can secure an interview, but verbal ability, logical deduction, resume articulation, and conversational confidence are what crack the offer. LitCrack is custom-built to train our students at KLECET in these exact aspects.
